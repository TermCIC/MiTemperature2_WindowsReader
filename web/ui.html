ⓘ Quick Start Guide Hide
1 Place ReadMi.exe in its own dedicated folder — all CSV files will be saved in the same folder as the app.
2 Keep your Xiaomi LYWSD03MMC sensor within 5 metres of your computer before scanning.
3 Click Scan for sensors — the scan takes 30 seconds. Keep the window open until it finishes.
4 When sensors appear, give each one a unique name and click Save — this prevents confusion if you have multiple sensors.
5 Click Fetch data on an available sensor. Watch the Activity Log on this panel — fetching may take up to a minute.
6 Once the log shows "Saved historical data to...", your CSV file is ready in the app folder.
